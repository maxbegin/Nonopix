How it works

Nonograms are logic puzzles where you fill in a grid to reveal a hidden picture. Here's the gist.

1
2 1 4 1
1 2 3 2

Read the clues

Numbers on each row and column tell you how many cells to fill in — and in what groups.

2

Use logic

Deduce which cells must be filled and which must be empty. No guessing needed — it's pure reasoning.

3

Reveal the art

Complete the grid and watch your pixel art come to life. Every puzzle hides a unique image.
